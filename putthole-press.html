DESCRIPTION
  • Putthole is an endless puzzle game in which your objective is to lay tiles and complete tracks. Completed tracks clear tiles from the board, cleared tiles award points, more tiles are worth more points. The game is over when the player has run out of available board spaces.

    Players must choose to take risks and attempt longer tracks for more points, or go slowly and take much longer to accumulate points. The game involves strategic tile placement and risk-taking while being dealt random tiles. While there is this element of luck, the game relies on player skill to handle unlucky draws and capitalize on the more fortunate.
FEATURES
  • Simple to understand, appeals to broad skill range
  • Endless play
  • No time limits - players play at their own pace
  • Either challenging or relaxing gameplay based on play-style
  • Game Center and Google Play Services leaderboards
  • No iAPs - All players share the same experience
