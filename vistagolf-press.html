DESCRIPTION
  • Vista Golf aims to be the best minigolf experience in your pocket. Play full 18-hole courses with our simple drag-and-shoot gameplay! Each week, compete for the top spot across three separate courses. At the end of each week three leaders are crowned, and three beautiful new courses will be opened.

    Also try Infinite Mode! Relax and play hole after hole, there's no limit to how much you can play.
FEATURES
  • 3 new courses every week!
  • Cross-platform leaderboards!
  • Achievements with unlockable badges!
  • Infinite mode!
  • Simple drag-and-shoot gameplay!
