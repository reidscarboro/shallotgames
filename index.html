DEVELOPMENT
Shallot Games uses a number of tools for creating our games, the foundation of which being libGDX and RoboVM. Thus far, all of our games have been created using these frameworks with the Java language. All of our game assets have been produced in-house with Photoshop, GIMP, and a few sound editors.


Please contact us if you might have any questions about the development and production of our games, I am always interested in supporting independent/hobby developers, and would love to help you with anything game-related.
